Dr. Rohit Tilak

Vice-President of TMV, and General Manager of daily Kesari Dr. Rohit Deepak Tilak, a distinguished scion of the magnanimous Tilak lineage, is the proud heir to the enduring legacy of Lokmanya Bal Gangadhar Tilak, the legendary freedom fighter and revered nationalist. Born into the 5th generation of a family steeped in the values of patriotism, education, and social service, Dr. Rohit Tilak personifies the harmonious blend of tradition and modernity that his ancestors championed.

A scholar par excellence, Dr. Tilak has amassed an impressive academic portfolio, holding a Ph.D. in Commercial Studies, a Master’s degree in Commerce and Journalism, and a Postgraduate Diploma in Business Management. His ,participation as a delegate in the esteemed Youth Participation in Social, Political, and Economic Front programme, organized by the Government of Washington D.C., further accentuates his global reach and commitment to progressive leadership.

Dr. Tilak’s exceptional accomplishments extend to the domain of sports, where he has earned the distinguished title of Judo Black Belt (2nd Dan) and has captured an unparalleled 14 consecutive Gold Medals at the State Level Judo Championships. A National Referee and a coach of distinction, he attended the prestigious World Judo Seminar in Tokyo in 2002 and participated in the Osaka International Friendship Judo Tournament in the same year, cementing his place as a stalwart in the world of martial arts.

A Leader Bridging Heritage and Progress

A visionary leader with an adaptable mindset, Dr. Tilak has held numerous key leadership positions across diverse organizations, imparting his wisdom and vision to numerous initiatives. As General Manager, Vice-President, and Trustee of various prominent entities, he has played a pivotal role in the functioning of the Kesari Mahratta Trust, which publishes the revered Kesari newspaper, founded by his great-grandfather Lokmanya Tilak in 1881. This publication has long been a powerful voice for social causes, advocating for justice, equality, and the empowerment of the masses. The Trust continues to uphold its legacy of excellence and relevance, with editions published in Solapur, Sangli, and Ahmednagar.

Dr. Rohit Tilak stands as the embodiment of a modern leader deeply anchored in his rich cultural heritage, a man whose dedication to public service, social transformation, and national progress reflects the timeless ideals of his ancestors while remaining attuned to the evolving needs of a dynamic world.
